Who We Are

Founded in Gilroy, CA, Brandwood Ag Services LLC proudly supports farmers across the Golden State. We serve a wide range of crops — from row crops and orchards to specialty varieties — offering products tailored to improve yield, soil health, and sustainability.

Soil Testing

Through soil health assessments and fertility management plans, Brandwood helps you maximize nutrient uptake, improve soil structure, and boost long-term farm productivity.

Nutrient Planning

From soil testing and nutrient recommendations to pest control strategies, our team works with you to develop a plan that best fits your farm’s needs, optimizing both crop health and profitability.
