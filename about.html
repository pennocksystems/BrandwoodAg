About Brandwood Ag Services, LLC

Rooted in California. Growing the Future of Agriculture.

Our Mission

At Brandwood Ag Services LLC, our mission is to empower California farmers with the tools and knowledge needed to cultivate healthy, high-yield crops through sustainable and science-based practices.

Our Story

Founded by Gilroy native Zach Griffin, Brandwood Ag Services was built on a deep-rooted passion for agriculture and a commitment to California’s farming communities. With multiple degrees and years of hands-on experience navigating the complexities of the state’s agricultural landscape, Zach brings expert insight and tailored solutions to growers across the region. Brandwood Ag Services exists to support sustainable growth, operational efficiency, and long-term success in California agriculture.
